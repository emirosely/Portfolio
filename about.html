About Me

I am a multi-faceted freelance creative based in Dublin, Ireland, providing front-end development, UI/web design, and overall graphic design/art direction services. I specialise in working with small business to create stunning websites that best meet their needs. Fluent in HTML & CSS, I can build out and customize any Shopify, Squarespace or Wordpress site.

My educational and professional background in Visual Communications has provided me with a strong foundation in design thinking and I love bringing what I have learned as a designer to the table as a web designer and developer.

You can see my design & art direction work via my design portfolio.
